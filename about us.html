About Us

Establishment and Location

SoleElite is a Grenadian base online footwear retailer, with its headquarters located in Byelands, St. Andrew. Although established in 2018, SoleElite roots date to 1999, as it has been selling shoes to its local customers since then.

What Do We Sell

Our store offer footwear from leading global brands such as NIKE, Adidas, Puma and Jordan as well as emerging brands. Very soon clothing for both adult male and female would be available for purchasing.

Our Mission Statement

We remain committed to continue satifying the needs of the sneakers lover with the best quality and fashion shoes.
